Fashion Recommendation System


The goal of this project is to provide personalized product recommendations based on user-uploaded images, rather than relying on their purchase history. The conventional recommendation systems fail to cater to users' immediate needs and preferences, as users are often inspired by something they see and wish to find similar products. To overcome this limitation, this project uses neural networks to process the input images from a pre-built Images Dataset and then employs a nearest neighbour-based recommender to generate the final recommendations. This approach enables the system to provide highly relevant and personalized recommendations to the user, matching their preferences and style. By utilizing state-of-the-art deep learning techniques, this project has the potential to revolutionize the way we shop and make product recommendations more effective and efficient.


The purpose of this project is to develop a recommender system that caters specifically to the fashion industry, leveraging the tendency of humans to be visually drawn towards attractive clothing products. With the advent of recommender systems in various domains, it has become imperative for retail industries to invest in the latest technology to enhance their business. Fashion has been an integral part of society for centuries and continues to be so in modern times. Women, in particular, have a vast product base to choose from, making it challenging to make informed decisions. Clothing and fashion have become a crucial aspect of modern-day living, with people often being judged based on their attire. The proposed recommender system aims to assist apparel providers in offering their customers a seamless shopping experience by enabling them to explore their entire product line and choose the products that best align with their personal style and preferences.


The objective of this project is to develop a robust and efficient recommender system for the fashion industry that leverages image-based recommendations, instead of relying solely on a user's previous purchases or browsing history. The project aims to overcome the limitations of conventional recommendation systems and assist users, particularly women, in making informed decisions when it comes to their clothing choices. The proposed system will use deep learning techniques to analyse user-uploaded images and generate personalized recommendations that match their individual style and preferences. The system will also provide availability information for the recommended products and alternative options within the same infrastructure. By enhancing the shopping experience for customers, the project aims to increase customer satisfaction, engagement, and retention for apparel providers. Overall, the objective of the project is to revolutionize the way we shop for clothing products and make product recommendations more accurate, efficient, and personalized.
